Line Standards
House rules for every cup.
  • → Swirl tight — peak above the rim, no sag.
  • → Toppings on top. Layer in the order listed.
  • → Drizzle last — finish with sesame for crunch.
  • → Weigh fruit on the prep scale, not by eye.
Cup & Lid
Branded Kefi 8 oz cup only. No clear cups, no swaps. Lid in bag for to-go.
Hold & Serve
Pour-to-pass under 90 seconds. If swirl drops, re-pour — do not patch with toppings.
