Modifier & Product Updates
Modifier Changes
Toasts — Egg Option
Now receive 2 eggs when added. The 1-egg option has been removed (used to be 1 or 2).
Breakfast Sandwiches — Egg Modifiers
Now forced: Fluffy scrambled, over hard, over medium — used to be over hard only. Now 2 eggs automatically — used to be 1 egg.
Classic Hummus Bowl — Protein Options
Now: Chicken, Shawarma, Lamb & Beef Kebabs, Grilled Eggplant — used to be all separate bowls.
Product Changes
Hardboiled Eggs → Soft Boil (Jammy)
